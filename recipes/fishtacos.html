Spicy Fish Tacos with Cabbage Slaw & Lime Crema

Description

This recipe was originally posted by the author Jennifer Pallian BSc,RD, in the website foodess. I can personally attest these spicy fish tacos with the fixins are absolutely mouth watering AND my newest go-to for fish tacos!
In only 25 minutes, you too can experience my favorite spicy fish tacos with cabbage slaw and lime crema! Keep reading to learn how!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs whti fish filets (tilapia, haddock, snapper, cod or sole)
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tbsp canola oil

For cabbage slaw

  • 4 cups loosely packed shredded cabbage
  • 1/3 cup chopped cilatro
  • 2 green onions minced
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 3 tbsp distilled white vinegar
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 tsp white sugar

For lime crema

  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 3 tbsp mayonnnaise
  • juice and zest of 1 lime
  • warmed corn tortillas

Instructions

  1. Season fish on both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. In a small bowl, combine paprika, cayenne, garlic powder, and cumin. Sprinkle over fish on both sides.
  3. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a grill pan on medium-high heat.
  4. Add half of the fish and cook until edges are opaque, about 3 minutes. Flip and cook until fish flakes easily with a fork, and is just ever-so-slightly translucent. Transfer fish to a plate and repeat with the remaining fish.
  5. To make the cabbage slaw, toss all ingredients together in a large bowl.
  6. To make the crema, whisk all ingredients together and season to taste with salt.
  7. Enjoy fish in warmed tortillas with cabbage slaw and a drizzle of crema. Yum!
